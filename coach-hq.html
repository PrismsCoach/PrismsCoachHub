Culture (Company Wide)
The four values every Prisms team member — coaches included — is evaluated against, alongside measurable impact.
Excellence

Every internal and external representation of Prisms reflects Prisms' best-in-class product, brand and leadership. Every work product — from email communications, customer meeting / presentation, content VR module, Skill builder, CFU, toolkit, data report, web product, etc. — will have an established bar for excellence, product specs, and that bar must be maniacally advocated for and held, undoubtedly.

Velocity

Our goal is to improve the academic and life outcomes of every student in the US, and globally, at the fastest rate possible. We cannot wait. Every work product is completed / shipped with utmost urgency (even if we have to defy the laws of physics), for swift feedback and rapid iteration cycles.

Team

Every team member has a contribution required for the team to hit their goal. As such, every team member takes full ownership over their goals and contributions, managing up when other team members are not owning their component rather than just doing it, which hides organization issues, while never blaming others for not delivering their best work. We win as a team, and this means each individual achieving their goals, holding the bar for everyone around them, and picking up the work or fixing a problem even if they were not a part of creating it.

Character

Truth and Integrity — What we think, say and do always align with one another, and uphold the truth.

Respect — We respect the humanity in every human being. This is reflected in how we speak to, broach challenges with, share feedback with, and resolve conflict with each person (team member, partner, teacher, education leader) — truthful, authentic, direct, kind.

A balance of humility and will — We listen intently and reflectively as we take in feedback and hear customers' and/or team members' challenges, and respond and act thoughtfully with a balance of humility and will.

Our Why
What It Means to Be a Prisms Coach

Prisms coaches sit at the front line of a mission to break down barriers to access and build STEM proficiencies at scale, for every student, worldwide. Every visit, every Institute session, every district conversation is a chance to move that forward.

Being a coach means being a clear, energetic communicator who can upskill a teacher's confidence and practice through side-by-side co-teaching and real-time feedback. It means being a curriculum thinker who partners with district leaders to make Prisms fit the classroom, not the other way around. And it means being a community builder — fostering connection among the teachers and coaches in your region, and among each other.

Prisms Coaching Standards
Prisms Coaches Believe
  • We believe teachers are capable of being successful with Prisms.
  • We inspire, don't force.
  • We hold a high bar with authentic care.
  • We move with urgency in everything we do.
  • We push toward independence, every visit.
  • We do not blame. We do not make excuses. We elevate.

A Prisms Coaching visit should be felt — by the teacher, by the students, and by anyone in the room. Energy and presence are not optional. They are part of the standard.
