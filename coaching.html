What Coaching Looks Like
The Coaching Visit Cycle

Prepare — Review Teacher Dashboard data and prior notes to set a focus for the visit.

Visit — Support the teacher in the moment through side-by-side co-teaching, modeling exemplary lessons, and real-time feedback.

Follow up — Send a specific, warm coaching email naming what went well and one area of focus, and recommend a next content module or skill builder.

Log — Mark the visit complete in the Service Tracker with a Notes entry, so the PL team has visibility into every visit.

First Year Rollout
Logging Your Visit — Service Tracker Notes

Every coaching visit gets marked complete in the Service Tracker with a Notes entry. This is the one place the PL team can see what's actually happening in classrooms across every coach, every region — this year, implementation success depends on that visibility.

What to include — the module or lesson type, a sentence or two on what actually happened (not a transcript), anything you'd flag as an Area of Excellence or Area of Focus, and any follow-up for the teacher.
Used the Live Coach Tool? Paste your "What Went Well Today" / "Focus for Next Lesson" summary straight into the Notes field — no need to write it twice.
